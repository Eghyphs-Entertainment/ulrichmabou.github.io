About

Hello friend 👋🏽- Thank you for visiting my page. I'm Ulrich, just another human :)

I was born in Yaoundé🇨🇲 (Central Africa) on a sunny🌞Thursday of March. Leaving my home country in 2011 to embark on a journey of discovery, I have been fortunate to live in the beautiful cities of Cape Town🇿🇦 (South Africa), Accra🇬🇭 (West Africa) and Nairobi🇰🇪📍 (East Africa) so far.

I'm a well-rounded technologist and techpreneur/intrapreneur with experience in product management, operations and engineering. My current areas of focus include product management and data analytics.

I'm a hobbyist developer and storyteller. I seasonally write on Medium about life, tech, entrepreneurship, data and productivity. Some of my other hobbies are listening to music🎶(Afrojazz, Blues, Makossa, Rumba, Afrobeat...), consuming podcasts🎙, reading 📚, and everything soccer ⚽.
