Cool Projects
Tickomasters (2019 - 2020)
The World's first sales and ticketing solution with payment processings within WhatsApp.

No need to browse multiple websites or download another app, just open your favourite messaging app to access available goods and services. Tickomasters accepts both mobile money and card payments, and digital tickets with QR codes are issued within the chat session.

As the product manager on this project, I led research, design and development efforts to bring an MVP to market and acquire a set of initial users.
Development Of A Smart Real-time Counter For An OEE Application (2017)
The first real-world IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things) project I worked on. As a local market leader in the field of energy management, Powerstar has realized the opportunity to help some of their existing clients save more on their energy consumptions and optimize their production processes.

The innovative solution was a set of products and services that I contributed towards as a R&D technician. The first product was a smart real-time counter which acts as the edge device and sends sensor data to the cloud for analytics. The aim of the project was to help customers monitor and optimize their OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) in real-time.

By the time I left the company, I had successfully iterated and delivered on the first version of a working MVP.
