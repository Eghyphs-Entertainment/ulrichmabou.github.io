No need to browse multiple websites or download another app, just open your favourite messaging app, search for interesting events around your city, and seamlessly RSVP within 2 minutes.

Development Of A Smart Real-time Counter For An OEE Application (2017)
The first real-world IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things) project that I got involved with. As a local market leader in the field of energy management, Powerstar has realized the opportunity to help some of their existing clients save more on their energy consumption and optimize their production processes.

The innovative solution was a set of products and services that I contributed to developing during my last six months at the company. The first product that we developed was a smart real-time counter which acts as the edge device and sends data to the cloud for analytics. The aim of the project was to help the customers monitor and optimize their OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) in real-time.
